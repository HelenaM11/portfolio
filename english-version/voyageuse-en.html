Left, right, straight ahead, criss-cross — Two books, divided into two parts. The first part tells a story in the style of a graphic novel, while the second part serves as a visual reference and captures the events through photography. This stage invites the reader to dive into a marvellous story. A path guided by different cultures and treats as markers: the Chinese moon cake, the Japanese mochi, the Brazilian biscuit, and finally, the French macaron. Diversity is expressed: «Une lieu avec une variété de cultures!
