Portfolio Helena Müller
DE EN

With colourful paper and brought to life through stop-motion, I created a Brazilian garden party. Through fun and playful sequences, it illustrates how such a garden party could unfold. I added fitting lively music and let the self-made figures perform on the garden stage — flat in 2D, experimental, with a punchline at the end: «After a good party, the pure chaos remains.» — Cut!
