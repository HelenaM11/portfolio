We are the four-person imaginary graphic design studio Team Heija!!!! The task was to design a campaign for the daycare initiative, and we were aware that licensing the Lego brand itself could potentially be risky. Those who design and communicate should also take risks — so we did just that with bright colours, with the Lego figures reflecting the message: daycare for equality, equal opportunities, affordable daycare places and better working conditions.
