We've all been there: you collect recipes here and there, and then they end up buried somewhere in your mobile phone or notebook. I used some of this data to create a small cookbook with 21 different international desserts (what can I say im a sweet tooth!) that I've already tried myself! It also includes QR codes to YouTube videos as a helpful reference. Immerse yourself in the horizon of desserts in clouds of cultures.
